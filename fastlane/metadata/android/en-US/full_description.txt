A clean, modern, and lightweight Android widget that allows you to display and auto-refresh images from any custom URL directly on your home screen.

Features:
- Stream and update images in the background using WorkManager.
- Supports grid dimension custom aspect ratios (3x2, 2x2, 5x2, etc.).
- Image layout scaling adjustments (Stretch, Crop, Fill).
- Adaptive launcher icons with Material themed monochrome stencils.
- Customized frame borders (Dark, Light, Custom Hex color wheel picker).
- Feathered blur overlays to obscure background details with zero-halo edge blending.
